# RS-011 Governance Clarity And Impact

## Goal
Provide an explainer and decision matrix that distinguish hard constraints from advisory governance guidance and explain impact on ongoing development.

## In Scope
- Plain-language governance explanation.
- Rule impact matrix and behavioral implications.
- Clarification of what blocks progress vs what advises.

## Out of Scope
- Rewriting governance policy content itself.
- Automated governance enforcement changes.

## Observable Outcomes
- Governance intent and constraints are understandable.
- Continuous-development impact is explicit.

## Dependencies
- None.
